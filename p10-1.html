Random News Generator

Description

An ode to my time as journalist, the random news generator pulls up news about and from countries without filtering it through a particular portal regardless of its nature, left, right or center. For most of us, media is a formest connection to the rest of the world, and consequentlyt, has this unyielding ability to warp out belief systems about people, places, and things. Evidently, this has had drastic effects through history. Catering biased information to selective groups has already been proven to be a tool of mass destruction. I used this opportunity to create a simple interface that brings the user the 9 most recent news headlines from a range of news sources. While the lack of categorizing it a particular beat makes it seem random, it has quite the opposite effect. Perspectives are pieced together onto one canvas, regardless of their source and the reader immediately knows where it's coming from. This take on 'News at a glance' is my way of trying to chip away at the echo chambers we're subconsciously building for ourselves on account of a manicured digital presence. The constraint of the tool is set by the user when they enter the country they want to read news about.

    • Reflection

  • The sketch attempts to tackle the assignment's sousveillance prompt in the following ways:
  • a. People anywhere in the world, can access news about anywhere else in the world, which means that they are not limited by what news sources are bringing to them.
  • b. While I do acknowledge the irony of using big tech-built APIs to solve problems created by big tech itself, the fact that the the user is removed from its immediate influence in using an locally created tool brings us step closer to at least diversify the ways in which we're being delivered important world news.
  • c. When building the code for this, I only played the part of a "curator", not manipulating the data in any way but just choosing to represent it in the best way I could as permitted by my skillset. The interface is thus kept simple and non-distracting with all the power given to the text.
